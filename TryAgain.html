Since 1946

The puropose of the Howard University Campus Pals is to provide assistance to the new entrants of Howard University and to also assume the larger responsibility of effectively integrating them into campus life through the dissemination of information and education.

In the family

The Campus Pals were organized by Samuel B. Ethridge in 1946. Mr. Ethridge and some of his personal Howard friends would meet new students and their parents upon arrival at Union Station in Washington, DC wearing white carnations so that the new students would recognize them.

View details »

For a lifetime

After being recognized as a campus organization in 1947, the Campus Pals began to put on activities that would create fellowship between new students. The Campus Pal mission is to provide new students with the opportunities to get to know one another through fun events and history about our campus.
